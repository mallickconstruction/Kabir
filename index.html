CATEGORY

Coarse River Sand

Our Coarse River Sand is ideal for use in concrete mixing, foundation work, and other heavy-duty construction applications. Sourced from trusted riverbeds, this sand is screened to remove impurities, providing the ideal material for construction projects that demand strength and durability. Uses: Perfect for making concrete, foundation work, and other structural applications where strength is paramount.

CATEGORY

Fine River Sand

For projects requiring a smooth and fine finish, our Fine River Sand is the perfect solution. This sand is finer in texture and is most commonly used in plastering, tiling, and other delicate surface finishes. It ensures a smooth surface and is essential for high-quality aesthetic results. Uses: Primarily used for plastering, surface finishing, and preparing smooth surfaces for tiling or painting.

CATEGORY

Medium River Sand

Medium River Sand is the perfect middle ground between Coarse and Fine River Sand, offering versatility for a variety of construction applications. It’s ideal for general-purpose use in both concrete and plastering, providing a balanced mixture that’s adaptable to a range of construction needs. Uses: Suitable for both concrete mixing and plastering, it’s ideal for general construction purposes.
